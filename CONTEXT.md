# Context: Personal Portfolio

Glossary for Ahmad Faidzal Ibrahim's portfolio. Use these terms consistently in
issues, commits, content schemas, and code. Definitions only, no implementation
details.

## Content entities

- **Project** — A piece of work Afi built, shown in the Projects section. May be
  professional (e.g. Holigo) or personal (e.g. Photographyx). Has a title,
  category, description, contributions, links, tags, and optional images.
- **Contribution** — A single, concrete thing Afi did on a Project, shown as a
  bullet under the project (e.g. "Flight module: multi-city booking"). Distinct
  from the Project's one-line description.
- **Experience** — A professional role Afi held (company, location, period,
  responsibilities). The Experience section is the work history.
- **Education** — A school or degree program (institution, period). Includes the
  current BINUS degree and prior schooling.
- **Certification** — A completed course or credential, grouped by issuer
  (Dicoding, Udemy). Carries verification links, not stored images.
- **Gallery item** — A single showcase image with a caption, shown in the
  Gallery grid.
- **Skill** — A named technology or tool Afi works with. Skills are organised
  into **Skill groups** (Mobile, Web, Data & API, Tools).
- **About** — The identity block: name, role line, and bio paragraphs.
- **Site settings** — Cross-cutting identity data: social links, the CV file,
  and the Mandarin wordmark (开发者).

## Notes on vocabulary

- Prefer **Certification** over "certificate" or "course" for the credential
  concept.
- Prefer **Experience** over "job" or "work" for a professional role entry.
- A **Project** is the work itself; a **Contribution** is one thing done within
  it. Keep them distinct.
